About Us

Estes Park School District R-3 is a public school district located in Estes Park, CO. It has about 500 students in grades PK, K-5 with a student-teacher ratio of 14 to 1. The mission of the Estes Park School District R-3 is to meet the needs of the whole child. We will prepare all learners to excel as citizens in a diverse and technological society, through a curriculum that meets our ends and supports our global outcomes.

  • New Teachers

    We are excited to announce Jen as our EPES Instructional Coach. She comes to EPES with 20 years of experience as a teacher librarian and 3 years as a learning coach. She has spent her career up to this point in the Windsor School District. After relocating her family to Drake two years ago, she started looking for a work opportunity closer to home. Working at EPES is a dream come true, as her and her family are now full-time mountain residents! She looks forward to becoming fully enmeshed in this wonderful community. Elizabeth will be one of our new 4th grade teachers at EPES! She is originally from Cary, North Carolina, and graduated from Appalachian State University in May with a degree in Elementary Education. She has spent the last four summers working as a camp counselor at the YMCA of the Rockies here in Estes Park. The Estes Park community is so special, and she's so grateful for the opportunity to live and work here permanently. She is very excited to get to know all of our staff, students, and parents at EPES in the Fall!
